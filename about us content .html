ABOUT US


Welcome to Small Steps, a nurturing and inclusive Early Childhood Development center dedicated to fostering a love of learning in young children. Our passionate team of educators and caregivers provide a safe, stimulating environment that encourages social, emotional, cognitive, and physical growth. We believe in creating an inclusive enviroment that encourages interaction amongst our learners thus in return encoraging them to interact with learners from all walks of life.


Our Mission

Our mission is to support families in laying a solid foundation for their child's future success, through play-based learning, exploration and discovery. We believe in the uniqueness and potential of each child, and strive to help them develop into confident,curious and compassionate individuals
