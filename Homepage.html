Welcome

SmallSteps pre-school is the place where children will be treated with respect and love, Step into a world of discovery and growth with us! Our ECD is dedicated to fostering a love of learning and exploration in children.

SmallSteps pre-school was formed in August 2024, Early Childhood development is based at Gqeberha. Our school we teach Isixhosa, English and Afrikaans.
