COGNITIVE DEVELOPMENT

L A N G U A G E  &   L I T E R A C Y

The development of communicating with others languages and the ability to read and write

Activities include

  • Reading Aloud: Exposing children to various books and genres
  • Phonics and Alphabet: Introducing letter recognition and sounds
  • Word Building: Using blocks, magnets, or sand to form words
  • Dramatic Play: Fostering communication and role playing
  • Story Secuencing: Encouraging narrative skills
