DAILY ROUTINE THAT WE OFFER

07:00 - 08:00

RECEIVE CHILDREN/ FREE PLAY

Medicine received from parents needs to be written down in the medicine file for the parents to sign. communication between parents and techer regarding the childs night and special requests will be noted during this time.

08:00 - 09:00

BREAKFAST AND TOILET ROUTINE

The school provides cooked oats and juice.

09:00 - 10:00

MORNING CIRCLE AND EXERCISE PROGRAM

Bible and prayer, gross motor exerscises such as dancing and hopping.

10:00 - 11:00

WORK CYCLE

Individual exercises are presented to each child according to their individual weekly lesson plan , The Children choose their own work from different areas such as Language, knowledge and understanding of the work.

11:00 - 11:30

SNACK TIME AND TOILET ROUTINE

The school provides a bread and fruit snack and juice.

11:30 - 12:00

THEME DISCUSSION

Circle discussions of different topics such as different animals, different colours etc.

12:00- 12:15

OUTSIDE PLAY AND TOILET ROUTINE

Kids get to play outside with other kids at the playground.

12:15 - 13:00

STORYTIME

Alternate between English and Xhosa stories.

13:00 - 14:00

LUNCH TIME AND TOILET ROUTINE

The school provides a cooked, well balanced meal and juice.

14:00 - 14:45

NAP TIME

Kids get to rest after all the hardwork they've done.

14:45 - 16:15

TOILET ROUTINE / OUTSIDE PLAY / SNACK TIME

Kids play outside while being fetched by parents during this time.

16:15 - 17:30

INSIDE PLAY / ARTS AND CRAFT/ BLOCKS AND PUZZLES

Pick up time, School closes at 17:30.
